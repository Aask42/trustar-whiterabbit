Using WhiteRabbit
WhiteRabbit is an open source tool that allows security professionals to associate Bitcoin Addresses to known malware campaigns and track payments made to these addresses. We are hoping to provide a tool that can act as an early warning system for SOC analysts, threat hunters and researchers, and other defenders out there, that leverages the bitcoin public and immutable ledger data. Given the rise of importance of cryptocurrencies both in general and within the security context we propose that Bitcoin (and other cryptocurrencies) Addresses should be added as indicators to the “Pyramid of Pain”. The interesting aspect of cryptocurrency addresses is that they share characteristics of both hashes and TTPs.
